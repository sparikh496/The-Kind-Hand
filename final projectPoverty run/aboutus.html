About Us

Our Founders:

The founders of The Kind Hand are a group of four passionate girls. The group consists of Helen Ayele, Maleny Ruiz, Natalie Cruz, and Sonia Parikh. They met at a Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program in San Jose, Ca. As the end of their summer program came to end, they got together and brainstormed something that they were all passionate about. They decided to focus on the issue of poverty. Poverty really hit home with all of the girls as they researched more about it.
